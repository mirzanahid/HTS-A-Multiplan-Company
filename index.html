How does a Value-Driven Health Plan work?

What is Value-Based Payments?

With most plans, providers set their own prices — and some charge a lot more than others! HST’s Value Based Payments (VBP) is a transparent way to determine the price of service by reimbursing provider based on the value and quality of care that they provide. The process is based on Medicare prices plus a percentage.

Resulting in a lower price based on value with a baseline that is transparent and fair for all parties.
